Hi!

Sir Tim Berners-Lee invented the world wide web in 1989. Sir Tim is the director of world wide web Consortium (W3C ), a web standerds organization founded in 1994 which develops interoperable technologies(specifications, guidelines, software, and tools) to lead the web to its full potential. He is a Director of the world wide web Foundation which was launched in 2009 to coordinate effots to further the potential of the web to benefit humanity.

In 2001 he became a fellow of the Royal Socity. He has been the recipient of several international awards including the Japan Prize, the Prince of Asturias Foundation Prize, the Millennium Technology Prize , and Germany's Die Quadring award. In 2004 he was knighted by H.M. Queen Elizabeth and in 2007 he was awarded the Order of Merit. In 2009 he was elected a foriegn associate of the National Acadmy of Sciences.
